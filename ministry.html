BOYS' HOME

The boys home was started in June 2011 with five boys in rented accommodation at Punalur, Kerala. We have twelve boys in purpose- built house in Kerala .Our home is designed to run as a typical family home and is recognised by the government. These boys have access to good education, medical care and have a beautiful home to live in. They are all doing extremely well with their studies. All of them are coming from the poor tribal background, some of them are true orphans, some of them are semi orphans and some of them are destitute who lived in the jungle did not have opportunity for education and better living
