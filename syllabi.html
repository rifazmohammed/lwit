DEPARTMENT OF COUNSELLING

The Counselling courses will deal with three levels.

Deals first with the theological basis for biblical counselling through an examination of scriptures that uphold the sufficiency and superiority of God's word. Also addressed is the spiritual preparation of the counsellor and his/her readiness to assume the counselling task.

Covers the purpose of biblical counselling and provides insight into specific issues faced in counselling.

Provides on opportunity for participants to apply the knowledge and skills gained in level 1 and 2.
